SUPER BMX BASH

Welcome to Super BMX bash, the perfect place to test your skills for speed and agility.
Cycle your way round the track designed by the definitely-not-evil Facilitators in the shortest time possible.
Collect power ups to help you along and avoid pesky obstacles which will slow you down.

Instructions

: Hold the up key to move forward
: Hold the down key to stop
: Hold the left key to move left
: Hold the right key to move right
: Use the spacebar to jump

Hop or avoid banana peels or you'll lose control of your bike momentarily.

Avoid these barrels or you'll get a huge speed reduction.

If you have a need for speed then DO pick up these - they'll increase your speed for a pief period.

Definitely pass through all of these! You need each one to progress to the nest lap.
